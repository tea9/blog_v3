## mac下安装msf
[Mac下安装Metasploit](https://blog.csdn.net/myfather103/article/details/79212460)  

下载安装包  
http://osx.metasploit.com/metasploitframework-latest.pkg  
安装  

```
cd /opt/metasploit-framework/bin/
./msfdb init
./msfconsole

msfconsole
```
